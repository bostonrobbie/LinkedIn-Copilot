# Sales Navigator InMail — Hero Formula

A 5-paragraph InMail tuned for the BDR's voice. The opener is verbatim with the
intentional lowercase "its" (signature stylistic choice — do NOT correct to "it's").

## Subject

4-10 words. No em dashes. Anchor on the same hook content as the body. Examples:

- `SailPoint + AWS Security Hub` (activity-anchored, A++)
- `Two-plus years scaling Pathlock` (tenure-only, A)
- `SDSF meetup in Vilnius and Rocket monitoring` (activity-anchored, A++)

## Body structure (paragraphs separated by a blank line)

1. **Opener** — `Hi {First}, I'm at Testsigma, AI-powered test automation. {hook_sentence} Hopefully its not too much to ask how testing has been holding up at {Company} {tenure_or_activity_context}?`

2. **Connector** — `Reason I'm asking, when {company_category_summary} spans {tech_areas} all at once, the testing surface tends to multiply fast. {area1}, {area2}, {area3}, all hitting cross-stack scenarios at the same time.`

3. **Proof point** — `{customer_story}` (Rotate from CRED, Hansard, Medibuddy. Default: CRED — "As their FinTech app scaled, they got to 90% automated regression coverage with 5x faster execution once AI took over the test authoring and self-healing work their team had been carrying manually.")

4. **Capability** — `Testsigma's Agent does that across web, mobile, and API. Plain-English test authoring, AI self-healing when locators break, and coverage gaps surfaced automatically as new features ship.`

5. **Close** — `Curious if any of that sounds like what your team is running into at {Company}?`

6. **Sign-off** — `Best,\nRob`

## Hard constraints

- Opener uses lowercase "its" verbatim (intentional voice).
- Zero em dashes.
- Zero question marks except the explicit close question and the opener question.
- Subject 4-10 words.
- Body 700-1100 characters.
- Hook sentence + tenure_or_activity_context must trace verbatim to the captured LinkedIn evidence (Tier A = tenure, Tier A+/A++ = recent activity).
- No fabrication: LINKEDIN-QUIET prospects must use tenure-only.
